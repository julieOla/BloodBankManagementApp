Blood Donor Eligibility Results

1. Are you between 18 and 65 years old?

2. Do you weigh at least 50 kg (110 lbs)?

3. Have you been feeling healthy for the last 24 hours?

4. Have you been diagnosed with or experienced symptoms of any major illness in the past 30 days (e.g., fever, cold, flu)?

5. Are you currently taking any medications?

6. Are you pregnant or have you recently been pregnant (within the last 6 months)?

7. Have you had a tattoo or piercing in the last 12 months?

8. Do you have high or low blood pressure that is not controlled by medication?

9. Have you donated blood in the last 56 days (8 weeks)?

10. Do you have any chronic medical conditions, such as heart disease, diabetes, or liver disease?

11. Have you traveled to areas with a high risk of malaria or other infections within the last 12 months?

12. Have you had a blood transfusion or major surgery in the last 12 months?

13. Have you consumed alcohol in the last 24 hours?

14. Have you recently received a vaccination (such as for flu or COVID-19)?

15. Have you ever been diagnosed with HIV/AIDS or Hepatitis B/C?
